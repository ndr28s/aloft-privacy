Aloft Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 16, 2026

Aloft, also known as 비행멍, is an ambient sky app designed for relaxation. This policy explains what information the app uses and how it is handled.

Information We Collect

Aloft does not require an account and does not ask you to provide your name, email address, phone number, or other direct personal identifiers.

With your permission, Aloft may use your device location to render the sky for your area, including sun, moon, star, weather, and general place-based ambience. You can deny location permission; the app will continue to work with a default location.

How We Use Location

Location is used only to create the app experience, such as calculating the sky appearance, local time, weather-influenced clouds, and a general city or place label. Aloft does not sell your location data and does not use it for advertising.

Third-Party Services

Aloft may request weather or place information from third-party services. These requests can include coordinates or network information needed for the service to respond.

  • MET Norway weather API, used for weather forecast data.
  • BigDataCloud reverse geocoding API, used to derive a general place name and timezone information.

These services process requests under their own terms and privacy practices.

Data Storage and Retention

Aloft does not require a personal user account. App settings, such as sound or ambience choices, may be stored locally on your device if needed for the app experience.

Production builds do not send flight-visibility diagnostics. During limited internal or pre-release testing, Aloft may temporarily store the observation coordinates used by the app, device-reported elevation, current or selected-location mode, and aggregate aircraft filtering counts. These diagnostics do not include your Aloft Call Sign, message content, aircraft identifiers, or individual aircraft coordinates. They are automatically deleted after a rolling 14-day period and the collector is removed when the investigated issue is resolved.

Advertising and Analytics

The current version of Aloft does not include advertising SDKs and does not use third-party analytics SDKs.

Payments and Premium Features

The current version is intended to launch as a free experience. If paid features, subscriptions, or real flight-data features are added later, this policy will be updated before those features are released.

Children

Aloft is not specifically directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Choices

You can disable location access at any time in your device settings. If you do so, Aloft will use a fallback location or limited location-aware behavior.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as Aloft changes. When we do, we will update the effective date above.
