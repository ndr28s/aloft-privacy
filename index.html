Aloft Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 21, 2026

Aloft (비행멍) is a quiet ambient-sky app. This policy describes the data used by the current Android app and the optional Message Plane service.

Information Aloft uses

Aloft does not require a personal account and does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, contacts, or advertising identifier.

  • Location: with permission, precise or approximate coordinates and device-reported altitude are used to render local time, weather, celestial objects, nearby aircraft, and a general place name. Fixed premium places can be used instead.
  • App settings: visual, sound, download, notification, premium-entitlement, Message Plane, and wallpaper preferences are stored on the device.
  • Message Plane identity: the app creates an app-scoped call sign and a random authentication secret. The relay stores the call sign and a one-way hash of the secret, not the secret itself.
  • Messages: a recipient call sign, sender call sign, message text of up to 30 characters, and timestamps are processed when you send a Message Plane.
  • Notifications: if enabled, an Android/Firebase Cloud Messaging token, platform, and language code are stored to deliver Message Plane alerts.

How location and network services are used

The device may send coordinates directly to MET Norway for weather, BigDataCloud for reverse geocoding, and adsb.lol for nearby live-aircraft data. These providers receive the network information normally included in an internet request and process it under their own policies. Live aircraft positions are not relayed through or stored by the Aloft Message Plane server.

Aloft may contact its own relay for Message Plane delivery, route enrichment from public datasets, push registration, and optional ambience or cloud-asset downloads. Retired internal flight-diagnostic collectors have been removed; the current app does not upload observation coordinates or flight-filtering samples to the relay.

Message retention

Message Plane text is transmitted over HTTPS but is not end-to-end encrypted. A pending message is kept only until it is claimed by the intended recipient or for a maximum of 24 hours, then deleted. Delivered and expired messages are removed during cleanup. Avoid sending personal, sensitive, illegal, or confidential information.

Inactive push tokens are removed after up to 90 days and are also removed when Message Plane notifications or receiving are disabled where the device can reach the relay. The app-scoped call-sign record remains so the same local installation can authenticate and receive messages.

Photos and captures

Camera mode processes the camera preview on the device. Sky captures and camera composites are saved locally to the device's Pictures/Aloft collection when requested. Aloft does not upload those images to its relay.

Payments

Premium purchases and restoration are handled by Google Play. Aloft receives purchase and entitlement status needed to unlock features; it does not receive or store payment-card details.

Security

Aloft uses HTTPS for supported network requests, stores only a hash of the Message Plane authentication secret on the relay, restricts message length and rate, and lets users disable receiving and notifications. No internet service can guarantee absolute security, and Message Plane should not be treated as a secure messenger.

Advertising, analytics, and children

Aloft currently contains no advertising SDK and no third-party analytics SDK. Aloft is a general-audience ambient app and is not specifically directed to children. We do not knowingly request direct personal identifiers from children.

Your choices

You may revoke location, camera, photo, or notification permission in Android settings; disable Message Plane receiving; remove the app and its local settings; or contact us about the relay identity associated with your Aloft call sign. Some features will be limited when permissions or network access are unavailable.
